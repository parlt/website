What is PENT?

PENT is the token that powers the Pentagon Finance protocol. The PENT token enables the creation/fusion of nodes and gives rights to vote on investment and development proposals. PENT tokens are issued as a reward for creation nodes.

Creating/Fusion NODES

Pentagon nodes are what enable users to generate passive income. Users can chose between different tiers of nodes, all with different rewards and upfront costs. Once users accumulate certain amounts of nodes, they will be able to fuse their nodes to more powerful ones.

COMPOUND your rewards

The Pentagon Finance system is designed to incentivize compounding your rewards for maximum income. By continously creating and fusing nodes your rewards increase exponentially and you will in turn achieve powerful passive income.
